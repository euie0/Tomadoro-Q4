Things Well Done

1. All our members were able to participate in our stream as compared to last time where we only saw 4 members host our previous stream. Having more members meant there was more opportunity for different perspectives and different conversations.


2. Compared to our last stream plan, there was a big gap with the amount of time we were supposed to stream. Despite facing the same problem and not reaching the 1 hour and 30 miunte mark, we were still able to at least reach an hour worth of streaming. With 28 or so minutes from our First Stream and seeing 1 Hour from our Second Stream is something our group is extremely proud of.


3. As mentioned in the first point, having more people would cause for more discussion. Another aspect that made the stream feel easier for us was that there was no script and we were open to different conversations at any point but of course we'd still have t stay in topic. Without the script, we didn't sound to rigid with the way we spoke and the way we approached cerain question, instead, whenever someone threw in a question to the conversation, the answers really did come from the hosts own opinion and perspectives.


4. For the members of our group, we've gained confidence when it comes to talking in stream. Talking in front of an audience is scary itself but thanks to our First Stream, we've become comfortable in our own setup and learned how to not be afraid when it came to giving opinions during question and answer.


5. During our promotional plan phase, instead of being shy and deleting our posts, we were able to keep them up until now. We were also able to share our stream link to our family and friends to gain more viewers since we didn't do it last year.


6. With more proper planning we were able to create better sprites compared to our last stream. Instead of the simple colouring, we were able to create semi-rendered sprites for each of the hosts.


7. And lastly, we were able to follow our stream outline except for one part which was the commercial. We were able to add more questions to make up for the minutes reserved for the commercial.
