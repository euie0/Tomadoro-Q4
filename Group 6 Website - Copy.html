Stream Outline


First, we have to decide how long we want our stream to be but we also have to keep in mind that we are a new set of people introducing ourselves to the media so we should spend enough time with new viewers, enough time to at least let them know what we’re like, our content plans, introducing ourselves, and etc. We can also add music in the background of our streams, something that won't get us in trouble for copyright issues. Music and overlays that won't be too much of an eyesore and music that shouldn't overpower the speakers’ voices. At most, we can do an hour and 30 minutes worth of streaming in order to really bond with our viewers and create a lasting impression. If ever we go overboard our set time, that's also alright since we want to be able to talk and entertain our viewers. Topics: How Pomodoro helped you throughout school - storytime How Pomodoro has shaped you - storytime Tips for Pomodoro - storytime

Promotional Plan

After looking over our options, we think uploading a poster to different social media platforms would be our best option. Our poster can consist of a quick overview of who we are and what we plan on streaming. We should be able to create a poster that is eye-catching and interesting enough to have them tune in to our stream. Of course we'll also have to list down other social media platforms we're on and most importantly we should place where we'll be holding our streams.
